Crack two raw eggs into a bowl. 
Add milk and a bit of salt and pepper. 
Whisk with a fork until thoroughly mixed.
Heat a frying pan and add some cooking oil. 
When you can drop water onto the oil and have it fizzle, it's ready for cooking.
Dip bread into mixture being sure that it is completely coated with the egg mixture. 
Placed the coated bread into the frying pan and cook until golden. Flip the bread and cook the other side.
Remove the toast, coat with butter and maple sryup and enjoy!!
